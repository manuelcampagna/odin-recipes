Description

Pizza is a dish that takes his origins in Italy, more precisely in Naples. Pizza can contain a huge numbers of ingredients, allowing everybody to put on it their favorite ones.

Ingredients (1 pizza)

  • 250g flour
  • 150 ml warm water
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Preparation (Step by Step)

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and warm water. Add olive oil.

  3. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out the dough into a pizza shape
  5. Spread tomato sauce over the dough
  6. Add mozzarella and your favorite toppings
