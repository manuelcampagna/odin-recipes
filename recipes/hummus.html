Hummus

Home

Description

Hummus is originally from Middle East, it tastes so good, the ingredients are very cheap and the preparation is very fast, perfect if you want to invite your friends at home!

Ingredients (4 persons)

  • chickpeas 350 gr
  • 1\2 lemon juice
  • salt q.b.
  • paprika q.b.
  • oil (6-8 spoons)
  • water 20 ml

Preparation (Step by Step)

  1. Put the chickpeas into the blender with some water
  2. add the oil, the salt, the paprika and then the lemon juice
  3. blend all the ingredients togheter and look at the consistency, if is enough soft then your hummus is READY TO EAT!
