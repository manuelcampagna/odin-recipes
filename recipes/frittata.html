Frittata

Description

Frittata is a simple dish that have as main ingredient the eggs.

Ingredients (2-3 serves)

  • 4-6 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 cup vegetables

Preparation (Step by Step)

  1. Beat the eggs with milk, salt, and pepper
  2. Cook vegetables in a pan with oil
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and add cheese on top
  4. Cook on low heat until set, then finish under the grill or flip to cook the top
